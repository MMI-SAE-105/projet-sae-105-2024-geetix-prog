Vinyls

Vinyls are more than just listening media. Their warm and authentic sound has captivated music lovers for generations. Born at the end of the 19th century, they had their golden age between the 1950s and 1980s before being replaced by digital formats.

Today, vinyls are making a strong comeback, becoming collector's items and passions. Each record tells a story through music, artistic covers, and limited editions. Vinyl offers a unique sensory experience, where each listening session becomes a ritual, reconnecting the listener to the essence of music.
