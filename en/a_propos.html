About me

My name is Mathis Guellati, I am 18 years old and I am currently a student in BUT MMI (Multimedia and Internet Professions). I have always been passionate about computers, I discovered tools such as Photoshop and Premiere Pro when I was little. I created a YouTube channel through which I discovered a passion for audiovisual. I tested all the things that allowed me to be creative in my own way. I am a fan of design and I would like to become a UX/UI designer.

Explanation of the subject

My subject is to build a mobile site about the evolution of vinyls. Being a vinyl fan, I immediately had the idea to address this topic.

On this site, you can really enter the world of vinyls, especially with documentation in the form of articles, the first explaining the functioning of a vinyl, the second talking about the movie High Fidelity, the next one about the director Damien Chazelle, then the article about the book Rock Vinyls and finally the article about the album The Dark Side Of The Moon. You can also get information using the timeline on the page about the functioning of vinyls. Finally, it is possible to contact us using the contact page. Some textual elements on this website were generated by an AI.
